About Me

Hi! I'm a Computer Science student and self-taught frontend developer with a passion for creating clean, responsive, and interactive web experiences. I work mainly with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Angular, and modern tools like TailwindCSS, Bootstrap, and SASS, always aiming for minimalist yet engaging designs. My strengths are problem-solving, adaptability, and fast learning, which help me grow with every project I take on. My goal is to work as a frontend developer where I can combine pro gramming with UI/UX design to deliver both functionality and great user experiences.
